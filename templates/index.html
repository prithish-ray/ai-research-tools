Context

Why This Matters

AI does not solve the hard problem in investment research. The hard problem is structured judgement, auditability, and decision discipline. These tools are built around that reality.

Too many plausible answers

LLMs produce fluent, confident text regardless of whether the underlying reasoning is sound. In investment research, plausible is not the same as correct. The bottleneck has shifted from information access to output evaluation.

Auditability is not optional

Research decisions need to be traceable. Every tool in this system is designed to surface sources, flag confidence, and separate retrieval from reasoning — so the analyst can verify rather than simply accept.

Workflows, not point answers

A single LLM prompt is not an investment research process. These tools embed structured workflows: thesis formalisation, assumption monitoring, staged filtering, and explicit kill-switch conditions.

Hallucination risk is real

Every output on this site is labelled as a research aid requiring analyst review. AI-generated financial content carries hallucination risk on figures, dates, and attributed quotes. Source discipline is built into every tool.
